## Production Notes

- **Start with LOW saturation cases** — Israel Keyes, Lacey Fletcher, Jordan Brown, Curtis Flowers, Eric Frein. These have rich footage and almost no quality YouTube coverage.
- **MEDIUM saturation cases** are the sweet spot — enough interest to drive search traffic, not so covered that you're competing with established channels.
- **HIGH saturation cases** still work if you have a unique angle (deeper analysis, footage nobody else used, different narrative framing).
- **Florida cases** are easiest to source — Sunshine Law means everything is public record.
- **LVMPD (Las Vegas)** proactively releases — monitor their channels.
- **Chicago COPA portal** has thousands of cases with footage — mine it.
- **Pending trials** (Kohberger, Gilgo Beach) = get footage early, publish when trial drops for maximum search traffic.
