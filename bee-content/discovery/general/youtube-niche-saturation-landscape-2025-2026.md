| **Content sourcing difficulty** | Very Low (Reddit is public, infinite supply) |

**Top Channels:**

| Channel | Subscribers | Avg Views/Video |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| rSlash | 2.2M | 300K-600K |
| Am I the Jerk? | 1.8M | 200K-500K |
| Markee | 1.5M | 100K-400K |
| Reddit Aliens | 1.2M | 100K-300K |
| AskReddit (various) | 500K-1M | 50K-200K |

**Notes:** This is one of the most oversaturated niches on YouTube. Thousands of nearly identical channels use TTS voices to read Reddit posts. YouTube has been suppressing low-effort Reddit compilations since 2023. Only channels with distinctive narration or unique editorial angles still grow. CPM is low because advertisers view the content as low-quality. **Avoid unless you have a truly unique angle.**

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### Best Niches for a New Faceless Channel (AI-Assisted, 1-2 Person Team)

**Tier 1 -- Highest Opportunity:**

1. **Sleep Stories / Bedtime Content** -- Low saturation (3/10), growing demand, AI production pipeline is 90% automatable, extremely evergreen, passive income from replays
2. **Stoicism / Philosophy of Life** -- Low saturation (4/10), growing trend, public domain source material, AI narration works perfectly, educated high-value audience
3. **Business Case Studies** -- Low saturation (4/10), high CPM ($10-$20), AI scriptable from public financial data, proven format (Slidebean, Company Man)

**Tier 2 -- Strong Opportunity:**

4. **Mythology / Ancient Civilizations** -- Low saturation (4/10), AI image generation perfect for visuals, evergreen, educated audience
5. **Survival Stories / Near-Death Experiences** -- Low saturation (4/10), proven audience demand (MrBallen), less advertiser-unfriendly than true crime
6. **Data Visualization / Infographics** -- Very low saturation (3/10), viral potential, educated audience, AI tools improving
7. **Space / Astronomy** -- Low saturation (4/10), NASA footage free, AI visuals improving, high CPM

**Tier 3 -- Good Opportunity with Caveats:**

8. **Medical Case Studies** -- Extremely underserved (2/10) but requires medical accuracy (liability risk)
9. **Cybersecurity Explainers** -- Very underserved (3/10) but requires genuine expertise
10. **Prediction Markets** -- Extremely underserved (2/10) but very small current audience

### Revenue Optimization Strategy

For maximum revenue per production hour:
1. Target **high-CPM niches** (finance, tech, health, education) -- $10-$30 CPM
2. Create **long-form content** (20-40+ minutes) -- more mid-roll ads, higher watch time
3. Use **AI for scripting and voiceover** -- reduce production time by 50-70%
4. Build an **evergreen content library** -- videos earning revenue for years
5. **Multi-language dubbing** -- unlock high-CPM markets (Germany $38, Norway $43, Australia $36)
6. **Stack revenue streams** -- AdSense + affiliates + sponsorships + digital products
7. **Publish 2-4x per week** -- the sweet spot for algorithmic reach without burnout
