## German True Crime (14 channels, 279 videos)

### The Landscape

German true crime is **wide open**. The best-performing channel (ZDF True Crime) gets only 152K median views — that's **64x less** than Dr Insanity in English. True crime is the #1 podcast genre in Germany, but YouTube is massively underserved.

### Top Channels

| Channel | Subs | Median Views | Avg Views | Uploads/wk | Type |
|---------|------|-------------|-----------|-----------|------|
| ZDF True Crime | 50,100 | 152,792 | 194,330 | 2.2 | Public broadcaster |
| TLC Crime | 149,000 | 122,004 | 149,430 | 0.2 | TV network reupload |
| Lucia Leona | 498,000 | 89,420 | 107,169 | 2.2 | Creator (face on camera) |
| Deutschlands spekt. Kriminalfälle | 20,900 | 47,225 | 59,504 | 2.7 | Faceless documentary |
| Wahre Verbrechen - TC Podcast | 52,600 | 44,544 | 69,615 | 0.3 | Podcast video |
| True Crime - Convicted Perpetrator | 41,800 | 39,815 | 61,864 | 0.3 | Faceless narration |
| RTL Doku | 153,000 | 38,500 | 56,157 | 4.2 | TV network reupload |
| Kabel Eins Doku | 109,000 | 31,078 | 42,181 | 0.7 | TV network reupload |
| Real Crime Germany | 87,700 | 17,508 | 33,713 | 1.1 | Licensed content |
| FBI Files Deutschland | 41,000 | 10,818 | 17,971 | 0.8 | Dubbed FBI Files |
| Kati Winter | 245,000 | 8,384 | 17,556 | 3.8 | True crime podcast |
| Creepy WEG | 36,100 | 7,627 | 21,423 | 3.7 | Horror + crime mix |
| KRIMI WEG | 41,000 | 5,689 | 13,510 | 3.7 | Crime narration |
| Up Close - Deutschland | 29,400 | 2,001 | 11,020 | 2.6 | Licensed content |

### Key Findings

- **No German Dr Insanity exists.** The highest-performing channel is a public broadcaster (ZDF), not a creator.
- **Most German true crime is TV reuploads** (ZDF, RTL, TLC, Kabel Eins) — not original creator content.
- **The few original creators** (Lucia Leona, Wahre Verbrechen) are podcasts or face-on-camera, not Dr Insanity-style faceless narration.
- **Engagement:** 2.6% like rate, 0.27% comment rate — lower than other non-English markets.
- **Title patterns:** 41% use ALL CAPS words, 25% use numbers, only 4% questions. Short titles (≤30 chars) perform best.
- **Duration:** Average 44 minutes — German audiences accept long-form.
- **$38.85 CPM** means even 31K median views generates ~$663/video. A channel matching Dr Insanity's quality could realistically hit 500K-1M views/video and earn $10K-$21K per video.

### Key Findings

- **Criminalista Nocturno** is the clear leader — faceless narration at 302K median views, 3.3M subs.

Arabic true crime is the most surprising finding. Several massive channels exist (Kwili 6.7M, Abulsadiq 5.9M, Fares Ashour 5.7M) with strong per-video views. The market is more established than we initially assumed, but there's still room.

|---------|------|-------------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Fares Ashour | 5,690,000 | 2,116,576 | 2,086,790 | 0.9 | Creator (face) |
| Abulsadiq | 5,870,000 | 2,010,576 | 2,068,069 | 1.6 | Creator (face) |

| SAMEH SANAD | 2,090,000 | 990,219 | 982,423 | 2.0 | Creator (face) |

| Heba Soliman | 975,000 | 335,762 | 327,590 | 0.9 | Creator (face) |
| Neo | 290,000 | 274,778 | 307,952 | 0.3 | Creator |
| SULTAN | 736,000 | 180,523 | 178,719 | 1.4 | Creator |

### Key Findings

- **Arabic true crime is bigger than expected.** Fares Ashour and Abulsadiq both pull 2M+ median views — only Dr Insanity and EXPLORE WITH US beat them globally.
- **Most top channels are face-on-camera** — very few faceless narration channels. This is the gap.

- **Title patterns:** 100% statements (zero questions), short titles (≤30 chars) perform dramatically better (1.84M median vs overall 178K), very few numbers used. Arabic audiences prefer concise, direct titles.

- **The faceless narration gap is real.** Top Arabic channels are personality-driven. A Dr Insanity-style faceless channel would be differentiated.
- **Gulf CPM ($15-25)** makes revenue attractive, but audience distribution matters — not all Arabic viewers are from Gulf states.

## Strategic Recommendations

### Priority 1: German (Score: 10/10)

**Why:** Highest CPM on YouTube ($38.85), virtually no faceless true crime competition, proven demand (true crime = #1 podcast genre in Germany), only 152K median views at the top means massive room to grow.

**Action:**
- Create Dr Insanity-style faceless narration channel in German
- Cover both German crime cases AND international cases dubbed into German
- Target 35-45 minute videos, short titles (≤30 chars), minimal emoji
- Use ElevenLabs for German AI voiceover
- Even 100K views/video = $2,141/video at German CPM

### Priority 2: Arabic (Score: 8/10)

**Why:** Massive audience (150M+), high Gulf CPM ($15-25), few faceless narration channels (top channels are personality-driven).

**Action:**
- Faceless narration would be differentiated from existing face-on-camera leaders
- Short titles (≤30 chars), 30-35 minute videos
- Cover international crime cases — Arabic audiences watch translated content
- Cultural sensitivity: avoid political/religious cases
- Target Gulf audience for highest CPM

### Priority 3: French (Score: 7/10)

**Why:** Good CPM ($10-15), large audience, receptive to international stories. McSkyz has proven the model but ceiling isn't reached.

**Action:**
- Compete below McSkyz initially, differentiate with production quality
- French audiences accept long videos (52 min avg)
- Use list-format titles, some ALL CAPS allowed
- Cover both French and international cases

### Skip for Now: Spanish & Portuguese

- **Spanish:** Already competitive (Criminalista Nocturno at 3.3M subs), lower CPM, English channels already dubbing into Spanish
- **Portuguese:** Too small market, too low CPM. Add as supplementary dub only.
